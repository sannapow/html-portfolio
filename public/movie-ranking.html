The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

(Which is really hard because I'm not a huge film buff...)

Best new movie: Barbie

I wasn't expecting much here but it had me laughing all the way through. Take that, patriarchy!

Best old movie: To Have and Have Not

Humphrey Bogart and Lauren Bacall at their finest.

Best non-English language movie: Alaipayuthey

Classic Tamil film. Amazing soundtrack by A. R. Rahman. I first saw this film at just the right age.
